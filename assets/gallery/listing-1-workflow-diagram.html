AI Lead Research Agent — System Workflow
HOW IT WORKS
From your prospect list to qualified leads with personalised outreach — automatically
01
Step 01
PROVIDE YOUR PROSPECT LIST
Share your prospect list and qualification criteria — industry, company size, role, and what a good fit looks like.
02
Step 02
AI REVIEWS AND SCORES EACH PROSPECT
The agent reviews each prospect in your list and scores them against your criteria. No manual sorting needed.
03
Step 03
QUALIFIES AGAINST YOUR CRITERIA
Each prospect is scored against your ICP. Anyone who doesn't fit is filtered out. You only see leads worth your time.
04
Step 04
DRAFTS PERSONALISED OUTREACH
For every qualified lead, the agent writes a personalised message referencing their specific situation. Not a template blast.
05
Output
LEADS + DRAFTS DELIVERED
A clean list of qualified leads, each with a personalised draft ready to review, approve, and send.
